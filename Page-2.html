    BMSCE, BANGLORE

                         Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah Institution of Engineering (BMSCE), also known as B.M.S. College of Engineering, is a private engineering college in Basavanagudi, Bangalore. The college was founded by Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah in 1946 and the B.M.S Educational Trust is in charge of its administration. BMS College of Engineering offers courses in the UG, PG, and PhD levels. B.E. is the flagship program of BMSCE. The institute also offers PhD and MSc (Engineering by Research) programs. 14 departments have been designated as Research Centers. Admissions to all the courses offered at BMSCE are on the basis of various state and national-level entrance tests. These include PGCET, KMAT, MAT, GATE, etc.There are additional counseling sessions for seat distribution in some courses. Students must register for a course twice to get admission at BMSCE, once at the entrance exam’s official website and next at the official website of BMSCE.

BMS College of Engineering Courses and Fees

BMSCE offers full-time programs at the UG, PG, and PhD levels. B.E. programs are available in 17 specializations, M.Tech programs in 13 specializations, and PhD/M.Sc (Engg.) is available in 14 departments. B.E. is a 4-year program, while M.Tech lasts for 2 years. In addition to these, the institute now provides full-time MBA and MCA programs. Registration for admission to MBA and MCA programs begins in January. The UG application procedure, however, begins in February. Each program's admission is based on merit and entrance exam results. BMS College UG Ad​mission 2023
BMSCE offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech.) / Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) and Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch.) degrees at undergraduate level. The basic eligibility to these courses is having passed 10+2 or an equivalent examination. B.Arch. is a 5 year course and B.Tech./B.E. degrees have a 4 year duration. Candidates can get the application form from the college’s official website. After filling the form, the candidates have to send the form along with the application fee to BMS Educational Trust, Bull Temple Road, Bangalore.

The B.Tech/B.E. degree is offered in the following specializations:

  • Biotechnology
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Electrical and Electronics
  • Electronics and Communication
  • Industrial Engineering and Management
  • Information Science and Engineering
  • Instrumentation Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Medical Electronics
  • Telecommunication Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

The admissions to B.Tech and B.E. courses are granted on the basis of candidate’s merit in Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka Undergraduate Entrance Test (COMEDK UGET). The entrance test will have 180 multiple choice questions of 1 mark each. The exam will have three sections: Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. Each section will have 60 questions each. The candidates will get 3 hours to complete the exam. There will be no negative marking. However, in the event of a tie in the test score the principle of least negative responses (Number of wrong answers) will be adopted. In addition, if required, other methods may be adopted to break ties. The entrance test is based on the existing syllabus of 10 + 2 / 12th STD / 2nd PUC.There shall be no Entrance Test for candidates seeking admission to B.Arch Course. Candidates seeking admission to B. Arch course shall submit separate application form for counseling as per the notification on COMEDK website (will be made available before counseling).

BMSCE Scholarship

BMS College of Engineering offers two types of scholarships- Government scholarships and Private Scholarships. They fall under the following categories-

Government Scholarships

  • National Merit Scholarship
  • Karnataka Minorities Scholarship
  • Defense Scholarship
  • Govt. of India Scholarship for disabled students.
