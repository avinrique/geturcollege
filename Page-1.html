VIT , Vellore 

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private research university established in 1984 located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. VIT Vellore is ranked 8th among the top universities in India as per NIRF 2023 report. VIT Vellore has been accredited with an ‘A++’ grade by NAAC. VIT Vellore offers 64 Undergraduate, 35 , 16 Integrated, 2 Research, and 2 M.Tech Industrial Programs. VIT Vellore conducts its own entrance examinations for admission to B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and PhD courses. VIT Vellore also accepts merit in National Level Examinations like CAT, NMAT, ATMA, XAT etc.  As per Stanford University Ranking 2022, 48 professors of VIT are among the top 2% of scientists in the world.

Admission to VIT Vellore B.Tech program is based on merit in VITEEE.
VIT Vellore B.Tech fee is INR 1.98 Lakhs for the first year.
For Admission to M.Tech and PhD programs, VIT Vellore conducts VITMEE and VITREE respectively.  
VIT Vellore MBA Admission is based on CAT/XAT/MAT/NMAT/GMAT/CMAT/ATMA scores.
VIT Vellore also offers online courses through VITOL.
VIT Vellore Placements 2023 is concluded with 14,345 offers. VIT Vellore's highest 

package stood at INR 1.02 CPA whereas VIT Vellore's average package stood at INR 9.23 LPA. A total of 924 recruiters participated in VIT Vellore Placements 2023. TCS emerged to create the national record as it alone made 3719 offers. VIT Vellore's top recruiters include Microsoft, Airbnb, Qualcomm, Adobe, Uber, Intuit, etc. The Vellore campus of VIT University is located over an eco-friendly 372-acre land in Katpadi, Vellore Tamil Nadu. Apart from the Vellore campus, VIT University has campuses in Chennai, AP-Amrawati and Bhopal.

Results

VITEEE Entrance Exam result as conducted (VITEEE  for B.Tech, VITMEE for M.Tech & MCA, VITREE for M.Phil & PhD) by the Institute, are displayed on VIT University Website. The Result displays the marks obtained in various sections respectively. Top scorers under 1 to 20000 of rank range & from 20000 to 40000 of the range are called for the counselling process at both Vellore & Chennai campus of the VIT University.VITEEE Cutoff Marks & Merit List:After the declaration of VITEEE result, the cutoff marks are displayed by the exam board. Division of cutoff marks depends strictly on the category of the students. Students can follow these simple steps to check their VITEEE result.
Initially, visit the home page of VIT University.
Now click on the link provided & enter Application number along with the Password (whatever you have entered at the time of registration).
Click on the ‘SUBMIT’ button & result will be displayed on the screen.
Students are advised to save & take the printout of the scorecard for counselling purpose.

Exams

VITEEE 2024: VIT Vellor is expected to be released VITEEE 2024 Application Form in the 2nd week of November 2023 at the official website - vit.ac.in. Interested candidates have to apply before the given deadline to appear for VITEEE 2024. A official notification will be released regarding the same at vit.ac.in.

As per VITEEE 2024 Eligibility Criteria, aspirants must have secured a minimum aggregate of 55% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology in the qualifying examination (45 % in case of reserved category). Candidates preparing for the exam are advised to go through VITEEE 2024 Exam Pattern. The exam pattern will be released along with the official notification. There will be 5 sections in VITEEE 2024 Syllabus i.e., Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Biology, Aptitude, and English. Candidates must choose to appear for either of the two groups of VITEEE 2024 - PCME or PCBE.

Admission

VIT Vellore Admission to its flagship courses takes place through entrance examinations conducted by the institute itself. Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) University is a private research University located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. VIT Vellore is ranked 8th among the top Universities in India as per NIRF 2023 report.  Moreover, VIT Vellore has been accredited with ‘A++’ grade by NAAC for the last 3 consecutive years. VIT Vellore offers 66 undergraduates, 58 postgraduates, 15 integrated programs, 2 research programs, and 2 M.Tech industrial programs.

  • Admissions to B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D. are held through VITEEE, VITMEE, VITBEE, and VITREE respectively.
  • Admissions to other courses such as B.Sc (Hons), B.Sc, M.Sc, BCA, BCom, MCA, and MDes are granted on the basis of merit. 
  • Every year, VIT Vellore releases 5 cutoff categories for admission to various BTech courses.
  • The first-year fee for the flagship BTech program is INR 1.98 Lakhs. 
  • VIT Vellore Ph.D. Fees is INR 43,000 for full-time candidates.
